## Key Learnings

1. **Design for Fabrication**: Some designs need modification specifically for successful fabrication.

2. **Support Strategy**: Understanding when and how to use supports is crucial for complex designs.

3. **Orientation Matters**: Part orientation significantly affects print quality and support needs.

4. **Iteration is Normal**: Complex projects often require multiple attempts to get right.
